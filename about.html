NEUROINFLAMMATION AND CHRONIC PAIN

About us

This IBRO School is designed to meet the need of young scientists/researchers in understanding the role of neuroinflammation in the pathophysiology of neurodegenerative diseases and chronic pain. The school would comprise of interactive lectures, on Neuropharmacology, Neuroanatomy and animal models of Neuroinflammation and chronic pain. There would also be hands on practical sessions on Neurobehavioral testing, immunohistochemistry, and PCR. Participants (Postgraduate students, Junior Faculties and Residents) for the school would be drawn from Africa.
